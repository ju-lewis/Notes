
>[!Cite] Rice's Theorem
> Every nontrivial property about program behaviour is undecidable

## Computable vs Efficient
Computable = finite time (*decidable*)
Efficient = (*typically*) polynomial time w.r.t input

**Examples**
- CNF Satisfiability
	- Computable, not efficient
- Integer Factoring
	- Computable, not efficient
- AI Planning
	- Lies beyond the class of NP problems
