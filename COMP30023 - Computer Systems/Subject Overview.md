
## Assessment Structure

**Weekly Quizzes**
- Worth 10% total
- Due at 11:59pm on the day of tutorial
- Quizzes require an access code given out at the tutorials (non-transferrable)

**Projects**
- Worth 30% total
- *Project 1*: Week 4 - 7
- *Project 2*: Week 8 - 11

**Mid-Semester Test**
- Worth 10%
- Happens in week 6

**Exam**
- Worth 50%
- Written, closed book
